# PawPal.ID Features

## 🎯 Core Value Proposition

PawPal.ID is a comprehensive pet care assistant that lives in WhatsApp, providing:
- **Instant AI health advice** for common pet concerns
- **Smart reminders** for vaccines, medications, and vet visits
- **Personalized tracking** for feeding, potty training, and health
- **Emergency support** including lost pet alerts

---

## 🤖 AI Health Consultation

### What Users Can Ask:
- **Food Safety**: "Can my cat eat tuna?" "Is chocolate dangerous for dogs?"
- **Symptoms**: "My dog is vomiting, what should I do?"
- **Behavior**: "Why is my puppy biting everything?"
- **Nutrition**: "How much should I feed my 3-month-old kitten?"
- **General Care**: "When should I start potty training?"

### AI Capabilities:
- Instant responses in English & Indonesian
- Risk assessment (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH)
- Vet recommendations when needed
- Context-aware based on pet profile (age, breed, species)

## 📝 Health Journal & Notes

### Note Types:

1. **Health Notes**
   - Symptoms observed
   - Unusual behavior
   - Energy level changes
   - Appetite changes

2. **Incidents**
   - "Ate a sandal" 🩴
   - "Ate a cockroach" 🪳
   - "Got into trash"
   - "Ate something toxic"

3. **Behavior Observations**
   - Excessive scratching
   - Limping
   - Unusual vocalizations
   - Aggression

4. **Food Reactions**
   - New food tried
   - Allergic reactions
   - Digestive issues

### Features:
- **Severity Levels**: Normal, Concern, Urgent
- **Tags**: For easy searching
- **Multi-user**: Owner and caretaker can both add notes
- **Timeline view**: See history at a glance
- **Export**: Share with vet

### Example Notes:
```
Title: "Excessive hair loss"
Type: Health
Severity: Concern
Content: "Noticed more hair than usual on the couch. 
Bald patches near tail. Started 3 days ago."
Tags: #hair #skin #grooming
```
